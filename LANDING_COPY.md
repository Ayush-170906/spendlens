## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup, no trial. SpendLens is free to use. We make money when we connect high-spend teams with Credex for discounted AI credits.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
A: Every recommendation is based on official vendor pricing pages, verified weekly. Sources are cited in our pricing data. The audit engine uses deterministic logic — no AI guessing on the math.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We store the tools and spend numbers you enter, anonymized by default. Email is only collected if you choose to receive your report by email.

**Q: What if I am already spending optimally?**
A: We will tell you honestly. SpendLens does not manufacture fake savings. If your stack is optimized, you will see a green "spending well" badge.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast. For teams with significant savings opportunities, Credex can often reduce costs further beyond what plan changes alone achieve.
