# Unit Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. A typical deal is a startup buying $500-2000/month in credits at a 20-30% discount. If Credex earns 10% margin on a $1000/month credit purchase, that is $100/month recurring or $1200/year per customer. Conservative LTV estimate: $800 (accounting for churn).

## CAC at each channel

| Channel | Estimated CAC |
|---------|--------------|
| Hacker News Show HN | $0 — organic |
| Reddit posts | $0 — organic |
| LinkedIn cold DM (time cost) | ~$5 per lead |
| Twitter thread | $0 — organic |

## Conversion funnel

- Audit completed → email captured: 25% (1 in 4 users)
- Email captured → consultation booked: 10% (high-savings cases only)
- Consultation → credit purchase: 40%
- Overall: audit completed → paying customer: ~1%

At 1000 audits/month, that is 10 paying customers/month at $800 LTV = $8000 new ARR per month.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

Month 1-3: 500 audits/month, 5 customers/month, $4000 MRR
Month 4-6: 2000 audits/month via HN + word of mouth, 20 customers/month, $16000 MRR
Month 7-12: SEO kicks in, 5000 audits/month, 50 customers/month, $40000 MRR
Month 13-18: Embed widget drives viral growth, 15000 audits/month, 150 customers/month, $85000 MRR = $1M ARR

Key assumption: audit-to-customer conversion stays at 1% and average LTV stays at $800.
